LUXURY Velvet Rose Eau de Parfum, Sensual Luxurious Damask Rose and Dark Plum, Long-Lasting | 50 ml
INR 7655

Product Description

Indulge in the luxurious allure of Velvet Rose Eau de Parfum, a sensual and enchanting fragrance crafted for the sophisticated woman. At the heart of this captivating scent is the rich and velvety damask rose, which blooms beautifully alongside deep blackcurrant and dark plum notes. The fragrance takes on a slightly spicy edge with the warmth of clove, creating an intriguing contrast to the soft florals. Finally, the scent settles into an opulent base of oud and patchouli, enveloping you in an aura of mystery and elegance. Perfect for evening wear or special occasions, Velvet Rose is a fragrance that exudes confidence, grace, and timeless femininity.
