LUXURY Enchanted Bloom Eau de Parfum, Fragrance Burst of Fresh Jasmine and Rose Petals | 50ml
INR 8549

Product Description

Step into a magical garden with Enchanted Bloom Eau de Parfum, a romantic and elegant fragrance that captures the essence of a blossoming paradise. The fragrance opens with a radiant burst of fresh jasmine, brightened by the citrusy notes of bergamot. As the fragrance evolves, the heart reveals delicate layers of peony and rose petals, creating a soft and feminine bouquet that is both timeless and captivating. The warm, velvety base of sandalwood and amber adds depth and richness, leaving a lingering trail of sophistication. Enchanted Bloom is perfect for any occasion, whether you're attending a formal event or enjoying a casual day out.
